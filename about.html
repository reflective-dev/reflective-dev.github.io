Who we are

We are a team of developers who care about building high quality, resilient software that solves real problems for people. With a history of shipping large-scale systems, we have experience teaching teams how to iterate quickly, reduce development risks, and operationalize software.

Meet the founders

Erik Hanson has 30 years of professional experience shipping high-quality, user-friendly software as an individual contributor, consultant, and hands-on manager. He has built web, mobile, and desktop apps in a variety of languages, and enjoys building user interfaces as much as building backends.

Eric Saxby ("Sax") has worked in a variety of fields, from e-commerce to health care to finance, at Fortune 500 companies as well as at scrappy startups. He has built software capable of handling data at a global scale, with the ability to change and adapt quickly to product demands.
